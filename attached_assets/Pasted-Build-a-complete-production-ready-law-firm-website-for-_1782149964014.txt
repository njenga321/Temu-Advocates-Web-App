Colors (use as CSS custom properties):
  --obsidian:   #111315  → primary backgrounds, nav
  --slate-navy: #1F2937  → card backgrounds, section fills
  --gold:       #D6B25E  → accents, CTAs, borders, highlights
  --ivory:      #F7F7F5  → light section backgrounds, body text on dark
  --steel:      #5B6470  → secondary text, captions, dividers

Typography:
  - Headings: "Cormorant Garamond" (Google Fonts) — elegant, editorial
  - Body: "DM Sans" (Google Fonts) — clean, readable
  - Labels/Caps: "DM Mono" — small uppercase labels only
  - Import both from Google Fonts in index.html

Voice Rules (apply to ALL written copy):
  - Short sentences. No padding.
  - No legal clichés ("trusted legal partner", "zealous advocacy", etc.)
  - No exaggerated claims.
  - Tone: strategic advisor, not a salesperson.
  - Competence implied, never announced.
  - Emphasize: clarity, judgment, discretion.

Tagline (use throughout): "Clarity in complex matters."
